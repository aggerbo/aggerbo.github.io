Limeline

We don't have any fancy algorithms or machine learning, claiming that we serve you the best content from your twitter stream. Nope, we simply go trough your feed and grab all the URLS and collect them in a little package, for you to consume without all the noise.

Why?

We found ourselves scrolling through Twitter, without any real value being created. But we also felt that we would lose out if we cut Twitter completely from our lives. Therefore we wanted to create a really simple app, that does nothing but pulling links from your twitter timeline. Simple? Yes… Effective? Indeed…

With the app you get all the latest articles, stories and great medium posts that you would otherwise miss if you weren’t scrolling twitter from top to bottom.

Consider it a sum up of the day of awesome articles and knowledge.

Now we want to be honest with you, this is a sideproject. This is something we create for ourselves but we want to share the app, because we think you could benefit from it too.

Therefore we created a small fee to support the evolution of the app and to help pay some of the bills that tags along on the journey. But fear not, we won’t charge you money until we think the app is ready for it, and actually adds value to your everyday life.

We are excited to have you on board!
